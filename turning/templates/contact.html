What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Fujairah and the surrounding Emirates. For projects outside these areas, please contact us to discuss arrangements.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency assistance for urgent industrial machinery and hydraulic system issues. Contact our emergency hotline for immediate support.

Can you customize machinery to specific requirements?

Absolutely! Our specialty is custom-engineered machinery tailored precisely to client requirements. We design and manufacture Brush Kart machines and other equipment based on your specific operational needs and usage conditions.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including cash, bank transfers, and checks. Payment terms can be discussed based on the project scope and can be arranged for larger contracts. Contact us to discuss payment options for your specific project.
