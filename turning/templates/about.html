Who We Are

Al Shamil Turning LLC is a well-established engineering and equipment manufacturing company founded in 2006. With over 20 years of industry experience, we have built a strong reputation for delivering custom-engineered machinery and reliable industrial services tailored precisely to client requirements.

Our expertise lies in the design and manufacture of Brush Kart machines, developed based on specific operational needs and usage conditions. We also undertake the fabrication and servicing of all types of industrial machines, along with light fabrication, welding works, and comprehensive hydraulic system design, manufacturing, and maintenance. Our technical expertise allows us to create, modify, and service any type of hydraulic solution, regardless of complexity.
